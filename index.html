The Problem

One home page. Same for everyone.

Most investing apps show every customer the same home page — the same tiles, the same tools — whether you're making your first SIP or trading options every morning.

Discovery breaks
Relevant products sit buried under widgets the customer will never open.
Messages feel like spam
One broadcast goes to everyone — driving fatigue and unsubscribes.
The right fit never meets

The Solution

The MO Personalization Suite rebuilds the home page around each investor — and tailors every communication to how they actually invest. One foundation, live on the RIISE app today.
